## Northwest

Here's the pitch for the main project I'm working on. 

The year is 1850 


The British Empire sends its finest expeditions into the Arctic in search of the Northwest Passage. You are among the sailors of the Royal Navy — disciplined, weary, bound to duty. You will endure cold that bites like iron, nights without end, and seas where silence breathes. To survive, you must cling to each other. 

 

Yet beneath the ice lies a prison older than the Flood: the abyss which harbors Abaddon, the angel of the apocalypse. Many ancient myths speak of him by different names, yet all proclaim a chained deity, unmaker of creation. His acolytes walk among you. They believe mankind is a plague upon the earth and that only by feeding him souls will the world be cleansed. If they succeed, the Destroyer will rise and bring the age of humanity to an end.
